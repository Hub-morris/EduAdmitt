# Recommended Programmes to Add

This document lists the programmes you should add manually to the EduAdmit system after removing seeded data. Add these programmes with the appropriate department and faculty values.

## 1. Bachelor of Computer Science
- Code: BCS
- Department: Computer Science
- Faculty: Faculty of Science & Technology
- Description: A comprehensive programme covering software development, algorithms, and systems design.
- Duration: 4 Years
- Fees: 120000
- Minimum qualification: C+
- Minimum grade points: 7
- Intake: January & September
- Type: Full-time
- Degree: Bachelor
- Overview: This programme equips students with foundational and advanced computing skills for the tech industry.
- Requirements: KCSE Mean Grade C+ with C+ in Mathematics and Physics/Computer Studies
- Modules:
  - Year 1: Programming, Mathematics, Data Structures
  - Year 2: Algorithms, Databases, Web Development
  - Year 3: Software Engineering, AI, Networks
  - Year 4: Capstone Project, Internship
- Career: Software Developer, Systems Analyst, Data Scientist

## Notes

- The rest of the data should be added manually through the admin interface or by creating your own seed/import script.
